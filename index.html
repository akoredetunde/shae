Our Mission

prioritizing prevention over treatment

We're Shathee, a mission-driven health-tech-start-up working to improve the health of individuals by providing evidence based high quality preventive care accessible to everyone on Earth, starting with Bangladesh. We also track markets of a patient's health over time - with the aim of intervening early to prevent complications before they arise.
